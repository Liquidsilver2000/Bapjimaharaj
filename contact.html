Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and booking process

How far in advance should I book a ceremony?

We recommend booking 3-7 days in advance for regular ceremonies and 2-4 weeks for weddings and major events. However, we can often accommodate urgent requests with shorter notice.

What areas do you serve?

We serve all 50 states in the United States. Panditji travels to your location, whether it's a major city or a smaller town. Travel expenses may apply for distant locations.

What are your fees?

Fees vary based on the type of ceremony, duration, and location. Basic poojas start from $300, while weddings range from $1,200-$2,500. We provide transparent pricing with no hidden fees.

Do you provide the necessary items for ceremonies?

We provide a detailed list of required items before the ceremony. In some cases, we can bring essential pooja items for an additional fee. We also have a spiritual shop for purchasing items.

Can you accommodate different regional traditions?

Yes, we are experienced in various regional traditions including North Indian, South Indian, Gujarati, Bengali, and Punjabi customs. We ensure authentic ceremonies according to your traditions.

What languages do you speak?

We are fluent in Hindi, Gujarati, and English. We also have working knowledge of Sanskrit, Marathi, Tamil, Telugu, and Kannada to perform authentic ceremonies.
